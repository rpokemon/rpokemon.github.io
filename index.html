Help Info

Usage: click on a flair below, using either of the two filters to narrow your search. Optionally enter a flair text in the field that says "Insert flair text here", this is the text that goes below your username in the user nameplate.

Once happy with your selection, click "Send Flair Request" which will open up a message form to /u/PokemonFlairBot, do not change anything, just click send. You should receive a reply from the bot within several moments notifying you that it has received your message and changed your flair.
